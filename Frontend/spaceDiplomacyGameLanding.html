The Emperor is Dead!

Seize the Opportunity

Space Diplomacy is a turn based political strategy game where players make and break alliances and conquer fleets so that they can control the galaxy.

"Gain power, hold power, and deny it to others." - Matt Colville on Diplomacy

A player wins if their faction controls enough Important systems. Each player begins the game in control over multiple star systems and multiple Fleets. During a player's turn they may Order each of their Fleets to Move to an adjacent star System or Support a Fleet's attack on an adjacent star system. If a Fleet recieves no orders, it Holds its current position. Players may also Mark Fleets for Decommission and systems for fleet Construction. Although players may submit or update Orders at any point during the turn, all Orders from all players occur simultaneously at the end of the turn.

If a player attempts to Move a Fleet into a system where another Fleet is Holding or attempting to Move into, then the move fails unless their advance is Supported so that they outnumber their opponent. When a Supported Move overpowers a fleet, that fleet will attempt to retreat to an adjacent friendly system; if it cannot retreat, the fleet is destroyed. If a player's fleet ends its turn in a system, then that player gains control over a system.

After every turn, Fleets are built or decommissioned until the number of Fleets equals the number of Important systems under a player's control. While no Important systems are marked for Fleet Construction and a player must construct a Fleet, the safest Important undefended system creates Fleet. While no Fleets are marked for Decommission but a player must destroy a Fleet, the safest Fleet is Decommissioned.
